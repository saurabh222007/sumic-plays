Sumic is a premium ad-free YouTube Music client for Android built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose.

Features:

- Ad-free YouTube Music playback
- Background playback with battery optimization
- Personalized Quick Picks and Keep Listening sections
- Offline caching and downloads for playback without internet
- Synced word-by-word lyrics with translation support
- Material You dynamic theming that adapts to album art colors
- Search for songs, albums, artists, playlists and videos
- YouTube Music account login and library sync
- Import playlists from YouTube Music
- Gapless playback and crossfade
- Silence skipping
- Audio normalization (EBU R128)
- Tempo and pitch control
- Sleep timer
- Discord Rich Presence integration
- Last.fm and ListenBrainz scrobbling
- Light, Dark, AMOLED and Dynamic themes
- Local playlist management
- Reorder songs in playlist or queue
- Customizable home feed with mood chips

Sumic is an independent third-party client. Not affiliated with Google LLC or YouTube.
